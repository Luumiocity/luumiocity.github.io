About

This portfolio is a movie based design study, in which we had to create a movie idea, and design advertisements and movie elements around it. My idea was of a movie in which a family is plauged by a "super hacker" of sorts, infiltrating their emails and social media, and making their way into their physical devices and using them against them as well. There are a few different styles shown here, as it was compulsary to delve into them, and these are mostly in the Intro sequences section. Some content is missing here, mostly the movie posters that were done, but these may be added later.
