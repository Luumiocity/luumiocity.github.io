About me

Hello. My name is Ben. I've been making creative media for five years now, ranging from videogames to posters, and most standard design medians. I've worked with a small range of topics, mostly involving technology, and some emotional and even "ethereal" topics, such as depression and existentialism. I've designed websites, photographed events professionally and even entered and won a few art competitions. There's been some downs as well as ups with my career but I think I still do a good job when I put my mind to it. But you can be the judge of that looking over this website and the main few portfolio's I've done from my last couple of years of highschool... and more to come.
